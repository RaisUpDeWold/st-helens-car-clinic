Our Services


Used Cars St Helens, Second Hand Cars St Helens,
St Helens VW Centre

When looking for quality used cars in St Helens your first choice should be St Helens VW Centre. We have a fantastic selection of quality used cars to choose from so we are sure to have the ideal second hand car for you at the right price. Based in Baxters Lane St Helens we boast of many satisfied customers from St Helens and the surrounding areas of Warrington, Wigan, Skelmersdale, Liverpool and beyond.

We specialise in Volkswagen and aim to pick out cars for you at a reasonable price. Our stock includes a hand-picked selection of used cars from Volkswagen and other leading manufacturers such as Seat, Ford, Chevrolet, Land Rover, Renault, Mini and Suzuki. We also stock a range of Volkswagen T4 & T5 Transporters. All vehicles are professionally valeted and come with full mot & service and 6 months' warranty.

Full dealer facilities are available including finance, warranties and part exchange.
