About St Helens VW Centre

Welcome to St Helens VW Centre website. My name is Craig McCarthy and I am the Manager here at St Helens VW Centre. We specialise in used cars and aim to pick out cars for you at a reasonable price. Dale Conroy
Garage Manager

Hi there my name is Dale Conroy, I'm the Garage Manager and Sales Person here at St Helens VW Centre.

I'm a qualified motor vehicle engineer and have over 30 years experience in the motor trade and transport industry. My duties are to ensure the vehicles either sold by us or brought to us for repair are done so professionally and as efficiently as possible.

Sales are also part of my duties and I'm here to assist the rest of the sales team help you, the customer and hopefully friend to be, find the right car for you and one that you are happy with. I consider myself to of had a good day when all of our customers go away happy.

So please come down to our garage and sales pitch and we'll be happy to help you with any enquiry.
