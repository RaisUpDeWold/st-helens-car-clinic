Meet The Team

Craig McCarthy
Director

About St Helens VW Centre

Welcome to St Helens VW Centre website. My name is Craig McCarthy and I am the Manager here at St Helens VW Centre. We specialise in used cars and aim to pick out cars for you at a reasonable price. Offering a personal, professional service focusing on honesty and integrity, and above all, value for money.

We stock a range of cars from manufacturers such as Volkswagen, Seat, Ford, Chevrolet, Land Rover, Renault, Mini and Suzuki. We also stock a range of Volkswagen T4 & T5 Transporters.

All vehicles are professionally valeted and come with full mot & service and 6 months' warranty.

Full dealer facilities are available including finance, warranties and part exchange.

Please explore our website where you will be able to browse all the cars we have in stock.

There's a contact us form after each car so get in touch if you would like a ring back or require more details about a specific car. Myself or one of my team are more than happy to help or why not pop in and see us, Amy makes a cracking cuppa! We're located not far from the Cinema in St Helens Town Centre.

Vehicle Sourcing

If a vehicle meeting your requirements is not in stock please contact me as I can source the car for you. Any vehicle sourced will have to meet my own high standards prior to being passed on to my customers.

Thank you for taking the time to visit our website, we look forward to meeting you, and being of assistance to you.

Garage Manager

Hi there my name is Dale Conroy, I'm the Garage Manager and Sales Person here at St Helens VW Centre.

I'm a qualified motor vehicle engineer and have over 30 years experience in the motor trade and transport industry. My duties are to ensure the vehicles either sold by us or brought to us for repair are done so professionally and as efficiently as possible.

Sales are also part of my duties and I'm here to assist the rest of the sales team help you, the customer and hopefully friend to be, find the right car for you and one that you are happy with. I consider myself to of had a good day when all of our customers go away happy.

So please come down to our garage and sales pitch and we'll be happy to help you with any enquiry.
